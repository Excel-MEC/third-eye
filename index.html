Event Description

An online photography contest spanning 4 weeks, where participants submit their photographs related to each theme. Each week will feature a unique and interesting theme based on which entries are accepted.

Rules

  • Entrants should be undergraduate students studying in India.
  • Entries should be submitted in digital format to the event mail ID, in JPEG or JPG format and less than 5MB in size.
  • Refrain from submission of watermarked entries.
  • Excel 2019 reserves the right to call for original JPEG or RAW files with unchanged EXIF for the purpose of authentication.
  • Heavy post processing is not allowed.
  • Excel 2019 has the authority to make all decisions pertaining to the event, and the decisions made will be final.
